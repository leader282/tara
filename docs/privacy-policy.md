# Tara Privacy Policy Draft

> Internal release-readiness artifact. This draft must be reviewed, hosted at the approved public URL, and linked from app store listings before any public submission.

## Product Scope

Tara is a private space for exactly two partners. Tara does not provide public profiles, followers, discovery, public sharing, dating matches, generic chat, monetization, or exact GPS tracking in the MVP.

## Data Tara Collects

- Account data needed to sign in and operate an account.
- Profile preferences that the user chooses to provide, such as display name, timezone, city/country labels, emotional tone preferences, and quiet-hours settings.
- Couple-space content created by the two partners, such as presence pulses, ritual responses, memory capsule metadata/content, media attachments, reunion countdown details, and private timeline entries.
- Device push-token data needed to send notifications when the user enables push notifications.
- Account safety records, such as data export and deletion requests.
- Operational diagnostics needed to keep the app reliable, with private content sanitized before logging or monitoring.

## Data Tara Does Not Collect Or Use

- Exact GPS coordinates or live location.
- Public profile, follower, discovery, or social graph data.
- Read receipts or partner-visible last-seen activity.
- Payment, subscription, or monetization data for the MVP.
- AI analysis of private memories, capsules, signals, rituals, or timeline content.

## Notifications

Tara notifications are designed to be privacy-safe on lock screens. Notification copy should stay generic and avoid private notes, ritual responses, capsule content, media paths, or exact location. Delivery should respect the recipient's quiet-hours settings.

## Account Deletion And Export

Users can request account deletion and data export from the app's account safety screen. Deletion requests are scheduled and can be canceled by the same user while pending. Production deletion processing must be verified in a controlled environment before public release.

## Store Submission Status

This document is not a live privacy policy by itself. Public App Store or Google Play submission remains blocked until the final hosted policy URL, App Store privacy labels, and Google Play Data Safety answers are reviewed and approved.
